Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is the ultimate family-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss. Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg, this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen mixed veggies
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients.
  2. Boil the vegetables.
  3. Heat a wok on high heat, pour in oil and stir in the vegetables.
  4. Add eggs and stir quickly.
  5. Stir in rice, add soy sauce and toss to coat.
  6. Drizzle sesame oil and toss again.
  7. Plate and serve hot.
