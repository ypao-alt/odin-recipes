Sugar Cookies

Description

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this top-rated sugar cookie recipe. It comes together with just a few ingredients you likely already have on hand â€” and the sugar cookies turn out perfect every time.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda and baking powder
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Beat sugar, butter, and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Mix flower with baking soda and powder in another bowl.
  3. Gradually blend the flour mixture.
  4. Roll the dough into balls.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden.
